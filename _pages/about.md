## About

I am a researcher at Inria (Defense & Security Mission) working on speech and audio processing. I develop explainable AI models based on phonetic features for Spoken Language Identification (LID) and Automatic Speaker Recognition.

My background is in experimental phonetics. My PhD (Sorbonne Nouvelle – LPP, CNRS UMR 7018) examined aerodynamic and acoustic variation in typical and Parkinsonian speech in women, with a focus on speech production mechanisms and hypokinetic dysarthria.

My research connects speech production knowledge with interpretable and robust machine learning approaches.
